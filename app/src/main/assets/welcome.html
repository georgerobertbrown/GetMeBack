Welcome to GetMeBack

Overview

GetMeBack allows you to mark a location and then easily get directions to return. This is useful for example if you park your car and go shopping and then forget where your car is.

Location

The app uses your phone's location services as well as Google Map's turn-by-turn directions to return to the marked location. Navigation modes are:
  1. Driving
  2. Walking
When the app is launched the first time, it will request location permission. Precise location mode MUST be selected for the app to function properly. Refer to Help for additional details.
